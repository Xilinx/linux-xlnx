* Analog Devices AD5820 autofocus coil

Required Properties:

  - compatible: Must contain "adi,ad5820"

  - reg: I2C slave address

  - VANA-supply: supply of voltage for VANA pin

Example:

       ad5820: coil@c {
               compatible = "adi,ad5820";
               reg = <0x0c>;

               VANA-supply = <&vaux4>;
       };
